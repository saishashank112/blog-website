## The Dawn of Autonomous Agents
By 2026, we've moved past simple chatbots. AI agents can now execute multi-step tasks independently, from planning entire travel itineraries to managing complex software engineering workflows.

> "The interface of the future is not a screen, but a conversation with a capable agent."

## Generative Video Becomes Indistinguishable
Hollywood-quality video generation from text is no longer a dream. Marketing departments are now creating highly personalized video ads in seconds.

## Hardware Catching Up
New AI-native devices are challenging the smartphone dominance, focusing on ambient intelligence and always-on assistance.
