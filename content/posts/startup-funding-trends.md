## The End of "Growth at All Costs"
The era of burning cash to acquire users is over. Sustainable business models are back in fashion.

## Vertical AI Startups
Broad AI models are powerful, but the real value is being captured by startups building specialized applications for specific industries like law, medicine, and construction.

## Seed Funding Remains Strong
While late-stage rounds have slowed, early-stage innovation is thriving more than ever.
